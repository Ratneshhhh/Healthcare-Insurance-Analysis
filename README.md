## 📌 Overview  
This project analyses healthcare insurance data to identify key risk factors and operational patterns. It is designed as a **product-oriented analytics solution** that enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions, improve risk assessment, and enhance operational efficiency.

The approach can be extended to **pharmaceutical and healthcare supply chain environments**, supporting better visibility and decision-making in regulated industries.

## 🎯 Problem Statement  
Healthcare and insurance providers often lack clear visibility into the factors influencing claim behaviour, making it difficult to identify high-risk users and optimise operational processes.

---

## 👥 Users  
- Insurance providers  
- Healthcare analysts  
- Business decision-makers  

---

## 🔍 Objectives  
- Identify key drivers influencing claim amounts  
- Segment users based on risk profiles  
- Provide interactive dashboards for decision-making  
- Support operational and risk-based insights  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics  
- Average Claim Amount  
- Claim Count  
- Risk Segmentation (BMI, Smoking, Diabetes)  
- Age-based Trends  
- Health Indicators (BP, Weight)  

---

## 📁 Project Structure  
- **Healthinsurance PowerBI Dashboard.pbix** → Interactive dashboard  
- **Healthcare Insurance Claim Analysis Project.ipynb** → Data processing & modelling  
- **README.md** → Documentation  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights  
- Claim analysis by BMI, smoking status, diabetes, and hereditary conditions  
- Age and gender-based comparisons  
- Interactive filters for personalised insights  
- KPI tracking for performance monitoring  

---

## 🧮 Data & Modelling  
- Data cleaning and preprocessing using Python  
- Feature engineering for risk factor analysis  
- Random Forest Regression model for claim prediction  
- Model evaluation using RMSE and R²  

---

## 💡 Key Insights  
- High BMI and smoking significantly increase claim amounts  
- Chronic conditions (e.g., diabetes) are strong risk indicators  
- Claim values increase with age  
- Distinct user segments exhibit higher risk patterns  

---

## 💡 Product Perspective  
- Defined key performance metrics to evaluate risk and operational behaviour  
- Designed dashboards to support data-driven decision-making  
- Enabled user segmentation to identify high-risk groups  
- Highlighted opportunities to improve risk assessment workflows  

---

## 🚀 Future Enhancements

- Predictive alerts for high-risk profiles  
- Improved dashboard usability for non-technical users  
- Extension to pharmaceutical supply chain use cases
